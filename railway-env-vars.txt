Environment Variables for Railway Backend Deployment:

MONGO_URI=mongodb+srv://YOUR_USERNAME:YOUR_PASSWORD@cluster0.xxxxx.mongodb.net/medical_assistant
JWT_SECRET=medical_assistant_super_secure_jwt_secret_key_for_production_2024
NODE_ENV=production
PORT=5000

Replace YOUR_USERNAME and YOUR_PASSWORD with your MongoDB Atlas credentials
Replace the cluster URL with your actual MongoDB Atlas connection string

After setting these variables, Railway will automatically redeploy your backend.
